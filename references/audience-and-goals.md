# Audience And Goals

## Resolve Early

- Who is the audience?
- What decision or action should happen after the deck?
- How much context does the audience already have?
- Is the deck for sales, strategy, training, reporting, or review?
- How much time is available?

## Practical Mapping

- Executives: faster orientation, fewer pages, stronger conclusions
- Prospects: pain, fit, proof, next step
- Internal teams: process detail and role clarity
- Training: buildup, examples, recap
