# Layout Patterns

## Reliable Structures

- Title + subtitle + three cards
- Title + left text + right diagram
- Title + two-column comparison
- Title + timeline band
- Title + chart + takeaway strip
- Title + summary and next step

## For Image-Based Slides

- Prefer fewer text regions.
- Put text on clear panels or bars.
- Avoid tiny floating labels.
- Use one dominant hierarchy only.
